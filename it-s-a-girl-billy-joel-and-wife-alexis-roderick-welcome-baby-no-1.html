It's a Girl!Billy Joel and Wife Alexis Roderick Welcome Baby No. 1

Publish date: 2024-07-08

Billy Joel is a dad — again!

The 66-year-old singer and his wife, Alexis Roderick, 33, welcomed their first child, a baby girl named Della Rose Joel, on Wednesday, Aug. 12, his rep confirmed to Us Weekly earlier today.

Billy and Alexis in October 2013.

Baby Della was born at 2:40 a.m. in NYC and weighed in at 7 pounds, 6.5 ounces. She is the first child for Alexis and the second for Billy, who has an older daughter, Alexa Ray Joel, 29, from his previous marriage to Christie Brinkley, 61. He and the former model divorced after nine years of marriage in 1994.
