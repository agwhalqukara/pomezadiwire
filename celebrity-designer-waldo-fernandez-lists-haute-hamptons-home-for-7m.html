Celebrity designer Waldo Fernandez lists haute Hamptons home for $7M

Publish date: 2024-08-01

He’s designed homes for Hollywood royals like Elizabeth Taylor and Angelina Jolie, supermodels like Kendall Jenner, “momagers” like Kris Jenner — and even the Academy Awards green rooms for Oscar himself.

Now, Los Angeles-based interior designer and celebrity decorator Waldo Fernandez is selling his own compound in East Hampton for a splashy $6.99 million.

The six-bedroom, six-bath home, with two powder rooms, is at 51 East Hollow Road. It features a chef’s kitchen with a butler’s pantry, along with an open living and dining room with a fireplace overlooking a mahogany deck and black granite infinity pool. 

A finished basement boasts a gym and laundry room. 

The main home, built in 2006, features verandas and storybook shutters. There is also a music room across a skybridge from the main house. 

The 1.2-2acre property is framed by bamboo groves. 

The listing brokers are Jack Pearson and Cee Scott Brown of Compass.
