Top Facts about Darrell Waltrip

  • Darrell Waltrip won 84 NASCAR Cup Series races.

  • Waltrip was inducted into the NASCAR Hall of Fame in 2012.
  • He retired from full-time racing in 2000.
  • Waltrip is now a commentator for Fox Sports’ NASCAR coverage.
  • He won the Daytona 500 once (1989).
  • Waltrip started his racing career at age 12 on go-karts.
