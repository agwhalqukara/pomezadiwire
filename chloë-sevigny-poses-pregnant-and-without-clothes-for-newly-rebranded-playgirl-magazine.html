Chlo Sevigny Poses Pregnant And Without Clothes For Newly Rebranded Playgirl Magazine

Publish date: 2024-09-13

Chloë Sevigny may have given birth to her baby in May, but she is pregnant and naked posing on the cover of the newly rebranded Playgirl magazine. What was that? You didn’t know Playgirl was around? You aren’t the only one. The magazine that originally launched in 1973 and printed its final issue in 2016, has returned and Chloë was thrilled to be asked to cover the magazine.

Playgirl now has an official Instagram account with photos from the new magazine that launched with its October 2020 issue posted on the site. Multiple photos of Chloë pregnant and nude appear on the Instagram account. It’s unclear at this time whether Instagram will remove them. Due to their adult content, those photos won’t be posted below.

Chloë shared one of the photos where she was dressed in a sheer nightgown over a bra and panties on her official Instagram account where she has over 800,000 followers. She stated the following.

When my friend of over 25 years, who I like to claim I introduced to his baby mama @mario_sorrenti took some pics to capture my very pregnant state weeks before giving birth for @newplaygirlmagazine
