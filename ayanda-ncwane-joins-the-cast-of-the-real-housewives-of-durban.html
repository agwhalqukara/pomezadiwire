Ayanda Ncwane joins the cast of 'The Real Housewives of Durban'

Publish date: 2024-07-29

- Ayanda Ncwane has been revealed as one of the cast members of The Real Housewives of Durban

- Ayanda Ncwane is the wife of late gospel artist S'siso Ncwane

- The Real Hosewives of Durban will make its debut on 1 Magic on 29 January

Ayanda Ncwane has joined the cast of The Real Housewives of Durban. Ayanda is the wife of late award-winning gospel artist S'fiso Ncwane.

The highly-anticipated reality TV show is expected to make its debut on 1 Magic on 29 January. Ayanda will be joined by other prominent women from Durban such as former President Jacob Zuma's baby mama Nonkanyiso Conco, Anne-Tonni Lodick, Nonku Williams, Sorisha Naidoo and Kgomotso Ndungane.

According to entertainment commentator Phil Mpela, Ayanda owns an artists management and entertainment business that is said to be keeping Sfiso’s legacy alive.

In other entertainment news, We reported that Khaya Dladla has bagged a new role on Season 2 of eHostela. The media personality took to social media on Sunday, 3 January to share that he portrays the 'macho' role of Nxebalendoda on the Mzansi Magic show.

The Gagasi FM radio presenter is popularly known as GC - a character he played on Uzalo. GC was a soft hairstylist who worked in a salon in KwaMashu, according to Uzalo's then storyline.

The star took to Instagram to introduce his new hardcore character Nxebalendoda to his fans. He shared a snap of Nxebalendoda with his post. He captioned it:

"Ladies and gentlemen allow me to introduce you to Nxebalendoda who will join you tonight at 8pm Ehostela Season 2... Your television will never be the same again."

Mzansi celebs such as Ntando Duma, Zola Nombona, Simza Ngema and Thembi Seete, among others, took to his comment section to congratulate him for bagging the new role.

The actor made his debut on the show about township life and the violent taxi industry on Sunday night.
