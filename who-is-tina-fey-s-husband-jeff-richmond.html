Who is Tina Feys husband Jeff Richmond?

Publish date: 2024-08-12

FORMER Saturday Night Live head writer Tina Fey and her husband Jeff Richmond have been married since 2001.

The couple have two daughters together, Alice born in September 2005, and Penelope Athena born in August 2011.

Who is Tina Fey’s husband Jeff Richmond?

Born January 7, 1961, Jeff Richmond is an American composer, actor and director.

He composed the music, and directed many episodes of the sitcom 30 Rock – which starred himself and his wife Fey.

Richmond, 60, also executively produced and composed the music for Unbreakable Kimmy Schmidt, another sitcom created by his wife.

Throughout his career, the 60-year-old has won three Emmy Awards for his production of the first three seasons of 30 Rock’s theme song.

In 2018, Richmond was nominated for the Tony Award for Best Original Score for the film Mean Girls The Musical.

How did Tina Fey and Jeff Richmond meet?

Fey and Richmond met in 1994, two years after Fey joined Chicago’s Second City improvisational theatre troupe.

She began dating Richmond, who was a pianist and later Second City’s musical director, that same year.

The couple dated for seven years before marrying on June 3, 2001.

Throughout her career, Fey has won a number of accolades, including, nine Primetime Emmy Awards, three Golden Globes, five Screen actors Guild Awards, seven Writers Guild Awards, three Producers Guild Awards and has even been nominated for a Grammy and Tony Award.

How many children do they have?

Richmond and Fey have two daughters together, Alice, who was born in September 2005, and Penelope Athena, born in August 2011.

Alice appeared in a cameo on 30 Rock back in 2012, where she played the role of Liz Lemon in the episode Mazel Tov, Dummies!.

Have Tina Fey and Jeff Richmond worked together?

Fey and Richmond have worked together in a number of projects.

The couple first worked together at Second City in 1995.

In 1997, Fey was hired as the head writer at Saturday Night Live, which Richmond later became the musical director of.

Following SNL, the pair collaborated on 30 Rock, Unbreakable Kimmy Schmidt and Means Girls the musical.

What is Jeff Richmond’s net worth?

Richmond has amassed a reported $3million net worth, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

His wife, Fey, reportedly has a $75million net worth, the outlet also reported.
