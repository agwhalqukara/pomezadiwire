Kate Mara, Max Minghella Split: House of Cards Star, Actor Break Up

Publish date: 2024-09-26

After four years together, Kate Mara and Max Minghella have called it quits. The two stars began dating in 2010, but recently broke up, multiple sources confirm to Us Weekly exclusively. 

"Max and Kate have broken up and gotten back together a few times over the course of their relationship, but this one looks like it's sticking," an insider says. "The relationship just ran its course."

Though they were photographed looking happy together earlier this summer, Mara, 31, and Minghella, 28, seem to have parted ways for good. The House of Cards star attended this past Monday's Emmy awards without The Internship actor by her side.
