3 Must-See DOOL Moments Week of March 18

Publish date: 2024-07-05

Days of Our Lives spoilers for the week of March 18 reveal there are three must-see moments. For one thing, Sloan Petersen-Brady (Jessica Serfaty) makes a demand of Nicole Walker-DiMera (Arianne Zucker).

Coming Up On Days Of Our Lives

Stefan DiMera (Brandon Barash) turns himself in to the police without alerting EJ DiMera (Dan Feuerriegel). Meanwhile, John Black (Drake Hogestyn) also decides to turn himself in to the police. However, Konstantin Meleounis (John Kapelos) could have other plans.

Nicole Walker-DiMera’s Baby Bond

DOOL spoilers reveal that Nicole has always had a special bond with Jude Brady. Of course, fans know that this is because Jude is Nicole’s baby, not Sloan’s.

However, Sloan has somehow managed to keep the truth from Nicole this far. Now that Jude’s christening is coming up and Nicole is back in Salem, Eric Brady (Greg Vaughan) faces a difficult situation.

Sloan Petersen-Brady’s Demand

Nicole offered the DiMera mansion for the venue. While Eric knew this wouldn’t go over well with Sloan, he really couldn’t turn down the offer. Sloan will play nice with Nicole in front of Eric. Sloan even allows Nicole to soothe and cradle Jude.

However, in a preview clip, Sloan demands that Nicole not attend Jude’s christening. It is teased that Eric overhears a conversation between Sloan and Nicole while planning the christening. So, maybe Eric finally hears how nasty Sloan is to Jude’s real mommy!

Stefan DiMera’s Police Confession

Days of Our Lives spoilers reveal that another must-see moment centers on Stefan. In a preview clip, Harris Michaels (Steve Burton) accuses Stefan of shooting him in cold blood.

Stefan doesn’t defend his actions, but tries throwing Ava Vitali (Tamara Braun) under the bus with him. Stefan exposes his sizzling passion with Ava. That’s when Ava punches Stefan in the face.

However, that isn’t all. Apparently, EJ searches for Stefan but can’t find him. The next thing EJ knows, Stefan has turned himself into the police and is speaking to detectives. So, EJ’s problems could multiply if anyone finds out about his shady shenanigans!

Days of Our Lives Spoilers: Week of March 18, 2024

Stefan won’t be the only one considering turning himself in. John also decides to confess to killing Konstantin Meleounis’ (John Kapelos) daughter. However, we have a feeling that Konny has more sinister plans for John than a prison cell.
