See Charles Barkley's Only Daughter All Grown Up Best Life

Publish date: 2024-09-18

In the 1990s, Charles Barkley was one of the top players in the NBA. At the same time, he was also the father to a young daughter. Maureen and Charles Barkley welcomed their daughter, Christiana Barkley, in 1989. Now, their only child is a 32-year-old who has a career of her own and is newly married.

While she keeps her life pretty private—she has no public social media, for example—Christiana's wedding was covered by the New York Times, her mother has shared photos of her online, and her famous father has talked about her in interviews. In fact, he shared quite a bit about the wedding, including his fear of participating in the hora. So read on to find out more about what Charles Barkley's daughter is up to today.

Christiana married her now-husband Ilya Hoffman on Mar. 6, 2021. The pair got married in Scottsdale, Arizona, where Christiana's parents live. In the New York Times article about the wedding, Christiana and Hoffman explained how they met.

Hoffman, who is not a sports fan, was invited to a bar to watch a Villanova University basketball game, and Christiana, a Villanova alum, was in attendance. While some people at the bar were talking about how Christiana is the daughter of an NBA star, Hoffman had a different response.

"I said, 'Oh, you mean the guy from Space Jam?' That's where I knew him from. Some people might have been like, 'Oh my god, Charles Barkley.' To me it was like, 'Look at this amazing girl.'" They continued to bond when Hoffman invited some of the group to hang out as his apartment afterward.

Charles approved of Hoffman and told the New York Times that he was "really emotional" when Hoffman asked for his blessing. But, there was one part about the wedding he was worried about. As the Times story notes, Christiana works for The Koppelman Group, where she is the director of writing and a senior account manager. The Koppelman Group is a college admissions consulting company that helps students get admitted to their top choice school.

Christiana and Hoffman live in Vermont. According to NYT, Christiana loves living near the mountains to hike and Hoffman enjoys being able to ski. Hoffman founded DemandByte, a marketing technology company.

Christiana graduated from Villanova University in 2011 and then went on to receive a master's degree in journalism from Columbia University in 2017. According to a page that appears to be her LinkedIn, in the time between attending Villanova and Columbia, she worked as a sales assistant at Turner Sports. Her father works for Turner Sports as an analyst on the show Inside the NBA, which airs on TNT.
