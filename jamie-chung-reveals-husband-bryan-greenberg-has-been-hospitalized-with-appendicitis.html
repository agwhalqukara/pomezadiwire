Jamie Chung Reveals Husband Bryan Greenberg Has Been Hospitalized with Appendicitis

Publish date: 2024-09-22

Bryan Greenberg is recovering in the hospital.

On Saturday (July 16), the 44-year-old actor’s wife Jamie Chung took to social media to document her trip to visit Bryan in the hospital as he recovers from appendicitis.

Click inside to read more…

“LOVE: Bringing your husband soup and clean clothes because he’s recovering in the hospital. Ferry ride to the hospital. Ready [or] not @bryangreenberg here I come!” Jamie, 39, wrote on Instagram along with a video that featured the Married with Children theme song.

In the video, Jamie shared some videos of herself making her way to the hospital before sharing a video of Bryan hooked up to IVs while walking around the hospital.

“MARRIAGE: Letting your wife sleep in your bed because she’s tired from the journey,” Jamie wrote at another point as she laid in his hospital bed.

“Love and Marriage. Jokes aside, Bryan’s on the mend!” Jamie captioned the video.

In a recent interview, Jamie opened up about why she and Bryan used a surrogate to welcome their twin sons.
