REPORTSharon Osbourne Trying to Get Daughter Kelly Osbourne a Co-Hosting Gig on The Talk

Publish date: 2024-09-15

Just months after she left Fashion Police, Kelly Osbourne may be joining the panel of a new talk show!

According to a new report, the 30-year-old TV personality’s mother, Sharon Osbourne, 62, is in talks to have her daughter join her on CBS’ hit daytime show, The Talk.

“Sharon is trying to cut a deal with The Talk. She wants to share the job with Kelly,” a source revealed.

Kelly and Sharon on The Talk in December 2014.

“Kelly is trying hard to land a full-time job after she very regretfully quit Fashion Police. She thought E! would commend her for taking a stand and giving fans what they wanted when she quit, but it backfired,” another insider close to the Osbourne family confessed.

Sharon — who has two other children, daughter Aimee, 31, and son Jack, 29, with husband Ozzy Osbourne — joined The Talk as a co-host for the show’s first season in October 2010. Now in its fifth season, the series additionally stars Sara Gilbert, Julie Chen, Sheryl Underwood and Aisha Tyler as co-hosts.

Sara, Sheryl, Kelly, Julie, Sharon and Aisha on The Talk in December 2014.

“[Kelly’s] team — primarily her mom — is working hard to get [her] her own show or a spot on The Talk,” the family source added to Radar Online of Sharon’s efforts to get Kelly a gig on the series.
