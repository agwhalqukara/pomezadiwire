NENE LEAKES MAKES BROADWAY DEBUT IN CINDERELLA

Publish date: 2024-07-27

Reality star NeNe Leakes had her husband and son on hand for her Broadway debut in Rodgers and Hammerstein’s ‘Cinderella’ last night in New York city.

Nene plays Madame, Cinderella’s mean step-mother in the hit musical. The Real Housewives of Atlanta star joins new castmates Keke Palmer, Joe Carroll, Judy Kaye, Ann Harada and more. You can see Mrs Leakes in Cinderella through January 3, 2015.

Nene and husband Gregg Leakes are parents to sons Brentt (pictured above) and Bryson.
