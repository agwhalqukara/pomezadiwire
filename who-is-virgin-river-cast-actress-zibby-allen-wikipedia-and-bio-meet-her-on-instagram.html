Who Is Virgin River Cast Actress Zibby Allen? Wikipedia And Bio -Meet Her On Instagram

Publish date: 2024-08-04

Elizabeth Zibby Allen, often known as Zibby, is an actress, musician, writer, and producer. Despite coming from Northern Ireland originally, she spent her formative years in the San Francisco Bay area. The current locations where Allen and her husband, Adam, spend time include Los Angeles, Vancouver, and Edinburgh, Scotland.

While she is not acting, Zibby devotes her time to her other artistic passion, music. Along with her musical partner, Ian Merrigan, she sings and was a member of the band “Socks and Chimes” in the past.

In addition to Grey’s Anatomy, A.N.T. Farm, The Twilight Zone, The Exorcist, Without a Trace, Bedheads, My Dad Says, For the People, The Flash, Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D., and Bates Motel, the actress has appeared in cameos on numerous other popular shows.

Additionally, if you watch the Hallmark channel, you might recognize Zibby from The Story of Us. The Good Doctor, Nancy Drew, Gabby Duran & The Unsittables, 30 Second Somebodies, and, of course, Virgin River are just a few of her most recent T.V. appearances.
