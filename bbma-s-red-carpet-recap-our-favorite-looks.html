BBMA's Red Carpet Recap: Our Favorite Looks

Publish date: 2024-09-08

Another award show, another red carpet. Or should we say, pink?

Last night the 2017 Billboard Music Awards took place in the ever so popular T-Mobile Arena in Las Vegas. And while we were suffering from some serious FOMO, we managed to create a montage of our favorite looks from the carpet. Check it out below!

Hailee Steinfeld


To whoever Hailee’s stylists are, we feel it is necessary to personally thank you. There has never been a time where this stunner didn’t slay any carpet she walked down.
Ensemble by David Koma. 

Jennifer Lopez

It’s really not fair to look THIS good at 47 years old. Someone leak her secret.

Machine Gun Kelly

We never knew how bad we needed to see Machine Gun Kelly in this suit until right now. Safe to say he absolutely smashed this look!

Camila Cabello

This is hands down one of our all time favorite looks for Camila. Simple, yet elegant. And absolutely vibrant.

Vanessa Hudgens

Host number one! It’s been great to see Vanessa come back to the music world looking as radiant as ever. Has anyone heard from Gabriella Montez? How’s she doing?
Gown by Marchesa. 

Bebe Rexha

Wearing a plain, yet beautiful black slip dress – Bebe proved that you can never go wrong with simplicity.

The Chainsmokers

Will we ever get over how cute these two are?! Probably not. Congratulations on your wins, boys! It’s been well earned.

John Legend

It’s a known fact that whenever John Legend attends an award show, you can always count on him to look fantastic. But if we’re honest, we were really missing Chrissy!
Suit by Gucci. 

Olivia Munn

Making her first red carpet debut after splitting with Aaron Rodgers, we believe it’s safe to say Olivia looks exceptional.

Lea Michele

Officially one of the coolest dresses we’ve ever seen. It’s almost as if it was created JUST for Lea. No words can describe how awesome she looks!
Dress by David Koma. 

Kate Beckinsale

This woman is a legend. End of story.
Dressed in Zuhair Murad. 

Florida Georgia Line

These two men won a total of SIX awards last night and we couldn’t be happier! They have our hearts, no doubt.

Ashley Tisdale

Another blast from the past it seems like! Ashley Tisdale stepped out onto the carpet in this all black ensemble to show support for her bestie Vanessa Hudgens.
(Sharpay may disapprove of the lack of pink.)
