Lilah Roloff Celebrates her Fourth Birthday!

Publish date: 2024-07-13

Little People, Big World spoilers reveal that Lilah Roloff has turned four years old! Her mother, Tori Roloff, shared quite a few photos and videos of the birthday celebration as Lilah’s friends and family took the time to make her birthday very special.

The Big Milestone

The day before Lilah’s fourth birthday, Tori shared a photo of her daughter sleeping. She captioned it, “The last time my sweet girl will go to bed is a 3 year old!”

She added some crying emojis to the post as well. Tori has always shared photos of Lilah and her other two children, Jackson and Josiah Roloff. She is very proud of her three children and it is clear that she loves them very much.

Celebrating Lilah’s fourth birthday was a pretty big deal in the Roloff house. There were plenty of videos and photos online, but fans did make a few observations about some family members that were not present at the birthday party.

Who Came To Celebrate?

When Tori’s followers watched the video of Lilah’s birthday party, they quickly noticed that Amy and Matt Roloff were not at the party, or at least they were not featured in the videos.

Tori captioned the videos, “She’e been waiting for this moment since she turned 3. She knew just what to do this time.” Tori captioned the photos of Lilah, “You are so special to our family. You are sweet, loving, and hilarious. You are the best little sister and big sister! You have grown so much this year with your confidence and being independent! I love watching you find new things that bring you joy! Thank you, God, for making me this girl’s mama!”

As Tori’s fans flipped through all of the birthday photos, plenty of them were shocked at how big Lilah is getting. Her fans gave her a lot of love for how adorable and beautiful she is and fans loved her twirling skirt!

This is a big milestone in Lilah’s life and it is clear that she is getting more comfortable in her own skin.

While fans of Little People, Big World sent their love, Tori’s sister-in-law, Audrey Roloff even commented about how she loved Lilah’s adorable sense of style and wished her a happy birthday as well.
