5 Mzansi celeb goddesses who look absolutely flawless without makeup

Publish date: 2024-09-08

We are used to seeing our celebs with perfect make-up so to see them without makes it a rare occasion.

Their faces are normally impeccable, with lipstick perfectly applied making their faces complete. It is now proof that our celebs are beautiful with or without make-up.

We has noticed that it’s not that people are against make-up, it is just that a natural look is a real power. It shows love for yourself portraying the natural you. It allows for your natural features to shine through.

Having to see airbrushed versions becomes a bit tiring, so to be able to see our favourite celebs braving their natural bare-faced online was pleasing and appreciated.

ZAlebs celebrated by recapping famous faces, taking a look at just how flawless they are without make-up.

Nandi Madida

Nandi Madida is such a good example of a natural glow. She does not wear weaves and is definitely the one to go to for natural beauty tips. Her skin is flawless and something we all strive for.

Jessica Nkosi

Jessica Nkosi is someone that so many envy. She is drop-dead gorgeous and most certainly does need any make-up as she was blessed with natural beauty.

Nomzamo Mbatha

Nomzamo Mbatha stars in The Coming 2 America. This star’s flawless skin is a characteristic she would never trade for anything. Her natural beauty has awarded her several campaigns.

DJ Zinhle

DJ Zinhle oozes confidence with or without make-up. Mama Kairo always looks good wherever she goes.

Minnie Dlamini

Minnie Dlamini our TV personality has a flawless skin and has her very own skincare products.
