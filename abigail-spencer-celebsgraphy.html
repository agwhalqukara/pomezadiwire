Early Life and Bio

Abigail Spencer was born on the 4th of August, 1981 in Gulf Breeze, Florida, United States of America (USA). Her father’s name was Yancy Spencer III and her mother’s name was Lydia Ann Brown. Her father was a surfer. Sadly, he died in 2011. She grew up with two elder brothers named Yancy Spencer IV and Sterling Spencer who were born in 1973 and 1986 respectively.

She studied at the Gulf breeze High School and then at Carnegie Mellon University.

Movies and TV shows

Abigail Spencer made her movie debut in 2001’s Campfire Stories as Melissa. After that, she appeared in various movies. In 2005, she was cast as Clarissa in the movie Fathers and Sons. Similarly, she played the character of Alice in the 2011 movie Cowboys & Aliens.

Moreover, she starred in 2012’s action spy movie named This Means War as a lead role. Her character’s name was Katie. Furthermore, she portrayed the character of Brenda Hesson in Chasing Mavericks (2012). 

Likewise, she has also played a brilliant role in an American fantasy movie named Oz the Great and Powerful as May. Some of her other fantastic movies are A Beautiful Now (2014) as Romy, This Is Where I Leave You (2014) as Quinn Altman, The Forger (2014) as Agent Paisley, and many more.

Abigail Spencer made her first appearance on television in All My Children (1999-2001) as Rebecca ‘Becca’ Tyree. Her performance was so good that she won the Soap Opera Digest Award for Outstanding Female Newcomer in the year 2000.

Some of her other popular TV shows are Suits (2011-2019) as Dana ‘Scottie’ Scott, Rectify (2013-2016) as Amantha Holden, True Detective (2015) as Gena Brune, Grey’s Anatomy (2017, 2019) as Megan Hunt. 

She is also famous for starring in the TV show named Timeless (2016-2018) as Lucy Preston.
