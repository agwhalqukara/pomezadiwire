Kim Kardashian Rents Out Staples Center for Kanye West's Birthday

Publish date: 2024-09-17

No biggie. Kim Kardashian rented out the Staples Center in Los Angeles for Kanye West’s 38th birthday on Monday, June 8.

“Kim rented out staples bitch!!! Him and his boys are going to ball!!!! Happy birthday Yeezy!!!!” Khloe Kardashian captioned an Instagram of West wearing a “West” jersey with his pals, including NBA stars Russell Westbrook, John Wall, Justin Bieber, Tyga, Pusha T., James Harden, 2 Chainz, and celebrity trainer Harley Pasternak, decked out in opposing “East” jerseys.

“Sisters @ the staple center!” Kylie Jenner captioned a fun photo of herself with Khloe and pal Malika at the fete.

In another post shared by Kris Jenner, John Legend is seen singing "Happy Birthday" for the birthday boy. 

Kim also shared a few photos on Instagram after the shindig.

Earlier in the day, West’s wife, who is pregnant with their second child, penned sweet messages for him on Twitter.

"Happy Birthday 2my best friend in the entire world! U R the most amazing husband &dad! U inspire me every single day,” North West’s mom gushed online, before adding: “You make all of your dreams reality & it's fascinating to be by your side & watching it all happen! You have a heart of gold! Have the best birthday ever! #SWISH"

Last year, Kardashian, 34, celebrated her hubby with a giant “Yeezus” cake and cheered him on from backstage while he performed three shows in Austin, Tex.
