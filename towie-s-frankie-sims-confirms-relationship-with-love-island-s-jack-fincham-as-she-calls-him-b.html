TOWIEs Frankie Sims confirms relationship with Love Islands Jack Fincham as she calls him b

Publish date: 2024-09-07

TOWIE’s Frankie Sims has confirmed her relationship with Love Island’s Jack Fincham as she called him ‘boyfriend material’.

Earlier this month the pair were seen sharing a kiss on a night out before going home together.

Taking to Instagram on Sunday, Frankie, 25, confirmed their relationship with two loved up snaps.

In the pictures, the Towie star sat on Love Islander Jack’s knee as he wrapped his arms around her waist and she wrapped hers around his neck.

With their heads touching, the new couple smiled for the camera and she captioned the shots: “Boyfriend material ❤️ that’s what he says @jack_charlesf we were together in 2019 & we reunited in 2021 xx.”

Their reality star friends immediately congratulated them, with Amy Childs writing: “So cute xxxxx.”

Love Islander Francesca Allen added: “I’m here for it” with clapping emojis while Towie’s Harry Derbidge posted a series of love heart emojis.

Frankie’s younger sister Demi wrote: “Yous have had a thing for so many years now, I’m glad yous have finally decided to take the next step!!! love yous.”

Earlier this month, Frankie and Jack, 30, were seen sharing a smooch on a night out together.

His night out with Frankie came just weeks after his split from her co-star Chloe Brockett.
